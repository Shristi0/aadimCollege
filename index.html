About Us

A Few Words About the College

Aadim National College is one of the best college in Kathmandu Nepal established in 2071. The college is located at prime location of Chabahil Kathmandu a walking distance from ring road nearby Chuchepati Petrol Pump. At undergraduate level, the college offers 4 years 8 semesters BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application) & BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies) degrees affiliated to TRIBHUVAN UNIVERSITY.

At AADIM COLLEGE, we recognize that higher education is a sound investment in the student’s future. Or enhance their promotion prospects or even chart a completely new career direction. Aadim college can help students achieve their ambitions. We focus on the key skills that employers need in the workplace, paving the way for our students to advance in sustainable, well- paid careers.
